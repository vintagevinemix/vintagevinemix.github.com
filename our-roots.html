Our Roots

At Vintage Vine we asked ourselves, why are all Bloody Mary Mixers filled with added ingredients that our pallets don’t deserve? Additionally, why mask our high-end spirits with low-end, unhealthy ingredients?

Two college friends set out to create the perfect Bloody Mary and Vintage Vine was born. Through years of taste testing and canning our mix in our Chicago kitchen we found that a perfect Bloody Mary starts with high quality ingredients and small batch production. We teamed up with a local, small run production facility that values taste over cost. Our idea became reality!

Begin with the Finest
then bring the Heat

Vintage Vine set out to find superior ingredients to make the perfect Bloody Mary. Not only have we combined all the common ingredients that connoisseurs look for, we turned it up a notch (not just our Vintage HEAT). Our All Natural, Gluten Free blend encompasses what true Bloody Mary drinkers expect, leaving out the unhealthy additives, bringing you back for more.

Jar with an Attitude
and a Purpose

The Vintage Vine Mason Jar brings Vintage Vine back to our childhood. It seals our All Natural ingredients in their freshest form. Our local hand craftsmanship, small batch production insures the integrity of our homemade recipe. Our reusable jar serves a global purpose by not wasting precious raw materials.
